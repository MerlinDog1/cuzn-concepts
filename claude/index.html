01 — Philosophy

Heritage is not
nostalgia — it is
foundation.

We believe the finest things are born where deep craft knowledge meets contemporary purpose. Each piece carries the weight of tradition and the clarity of modern design thinking.

Brass — the alloy of copper and zinc — has accompanied human ambition for five millennia. We honour that lineage while refusing to be bound by it.

◆
02 — Craft
I

Material

High-copper C260 brass, selected for its warm ochre patina and structural integrity across generations.

II

Process

Sand-cast, hand-finished, and aged under controlled atmosphere. No two pieces share identical surface character.

III

Detail

Precision-machined joinery. Chamfered edges at 0.8mm. Tolerances measured in hundredths of a millimetre.

"The object that endures is the one that was never hurried."
